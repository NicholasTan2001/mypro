Notification

This Notification feature sends an email alert whenever a login is detected on your account. It helps you stay informed of any account activity and provides an extra layer of security for your personal information. You may enable this feature to enhance your account's privacy and protection.

OTP Verification

You can manage your two-factor authentication settings here. When enabled, you will be required to complete OTP verification before successfully logging in. When disabled, you can log in using only your identity number and password without OTP verification.

Private Profile Visibility

You can adjust your profile visibility here. When enabled, only your friends will be able to view your profile. If disabled, your profile will be public and accessible to anyone.
